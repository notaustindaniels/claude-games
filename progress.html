2026-07-03 — Passes 3–9: physics corrections, R-evidence, R8; final 15/15

Major corrections this stretch (all found by measurement, not eyeball): (1) Tessendorf time-convention flip — the height-field NCC ground truth (app-measured, ncc 0.99) proved the sea travelled UPWIND; with the e+ikx inverse transform the hermitian pairing must use e−iωt. Waves now travel downwind. (2) Caustic sampler v-flip — the caustic RT's render path flips framebuffer Y relative to sampling (GL backend emulating WebGPU NDC), so the floor pattern was z-MIRRORED versus the wave field; one negate in the shared lightAt sampler fixed pattern orientation and made its motion physically coherent. (3) Uniform surface current (Stokes/wind drift, 2.6 m/s blackflag) applied at the sampling layer so waves, caustics and foam advect rigidly together. (4) Energy accounting for caustics: dense splat grid (768² = RT res) with peak-shaped, mean≈1 output — flat sea gives exactly neutral lighting. (5) The chop-fold family of artifacts: chop tapered to zero within 4–24 m of the eye, and the surface's above/below branch keys on fragment-evaluated camera submersion instead of frontFacing (folded faces can no longer flip shading). (6) Storm far whitecaps come from a fixed-LOD Jacobian (auto-LOD mip-averages fold events away) with a lace-jittered threshold and a 55–380 m window — an earlier version tiled visibly in M5 (pass-4/5/6; fixed in pass-7+).

G5b measurement methodology (final, documented for the record): the screen-space estimator is a multi-scale NCC over temporal-difference images (|f2−f1| vs |f3−f2|, 0.5 s apart) — static seabed albedo cancels exactly; the finest confident scale wins; the window center is clamped in-frame and the px→world conversion happens at the measured center. getFlowAt reports the app's own realized transport of the caustic-relevant band (cascade-1 LP height field cross-correlated 0.5 s apart at preset load, plus the surface current) — no spectral sign conventions, no hand-declared wind constant. Result: caustic pattern drift 11.2° off the reported flow at ncc 0.93. Gate threshold changes: none loosened; G5b additionally enforces an NCC confidence floor ≥0.12 (stricter than the contract).
